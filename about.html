About Us

Rooted in Faith.
Driven by Service.

Heaven’s Pantry was founded by Pastor Jasmine Darby Stroman of the House of Praise Non-Denominational Church of Deliverance with a vision to share the love of God through service and compassion. What began as a faith-driven initiative has grown into a community-centered nonprofit dedicated to fighting hunger and providing nutritious food to families, seniors, and individuals in need. Beyond distributing groceries, we are committed to restoring dignity, strengthening families, and building a supportive community through continued service and faithful stewardship.

Our Mission

Heaven’s Pantry exists to share the love of God by serving with compassion, dignity, and faith. Through continued service and opportunities to give, volunteer, and connect, we strive to strengthen our community and uplift one another in love.
